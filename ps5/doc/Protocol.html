Marshaling and unmarshaling


module type Marshalable = sig .. end
Send or receive marshaled messages through Reader or Writer

Both the Protocol.WorkerRequest and Protocol.WorkerResponse modules below include the Protocol.Marshalable interface, meaning that you can send and receive Protocol.WorkerRequest.ts and Protocol.WorkerResponse.ts over the network. For example, to send a WorkerRequest.t msg over the channel w, you should call WorkerRequest.send w msg.
